# robotic_tool

## 1. Data: contains the data collected from task 1
## 2. Force_data: contains the total raw data collected from task 2
## 3. task2_results: contains the proccessed results from task 2
## 4. XboxController:
### 1. xboxController2.py: code to received commands from xbox controller and excecute them calling arduino or kinova robotic arm
### 2. move_angular_and_cartesian.py and utilities.py: used to perform movements with the Kinova Robotic arm
### 3. instrument_test.ino: microcontroller (Arduino code) of the end-effector
## 5. force_test_plots.ipynb: processing of results and graph for the Force tests with each instrument
## 6. Frequency.ipynb: processing frequency of the signal while performing Task 2
## 7. FSR_calibration.ipynb: FSR calibration results and graph of weights vs voltage
## 8. kinematics.ipynb: kinematics calculations and experiment results
## 9. results_questionnaire_task1.ipynb: analysis of Task 1 results for time and plots of the questionnaire answers
## 10. Task2_experimental_results.ipynb: analysis and plots for Task 2 (all data)
## 11. Task2_mean_trial: analysis and plots for Task 2 (mean trial)
## 12. trigger_mapping.ipynb: mapping of pressed trigger vs the distance between the jaws
## 13. wrist_twist.ipynb: graph of the force vs angle per isntrument
## 14. Results.xlsx: results of the questionnaire, the time spent of task 1 and time for changing instrument
## 15. twist_test.xlsx: results of force vs angle of rotation while holding the three instruments when they are completely close and completely open
